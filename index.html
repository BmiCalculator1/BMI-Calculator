Welcome To Our Project

BMI Calculator

Curious about your Body Mass Index (BMI)? Our BMI calculator provides a quick and easy way to understand your weight status based on your height and weight. Simply enter your information and get your BMI number along with an explanation of what it means.

Our Features



Bmi Calculator


The Website calculates your Body Mass Index (BMI) accurately based on your height and weight input.



Weight Classification


It determines your weight class, categorizing you as underweight, normal weight, overweight, obese, or morbidly obese.



User-Friendly Interface


Designed with an intuitive user interface for a seamless user experience.



Health Information

Provide brief explanations or links to resources about the health implications of different BMI categories.



Real-time Calculation

Calculate and display the BMI instantly as the user enters their weight and height, without requiring a separate button click.



Privacy

Respect user privacy by not storing personal data unless explicitly authorized, and provide clear information about data usage and storage practices.
